Accommodations

Glen Oaks

Eco-friendly, mid-century modern rooms nestled in a redwood grove. Stay in the lodge or rent a private cabin.

Rates: $$ to $$$

VISIT WEBSITE

Lucia Lodge

Constructed in the 1930's by a pioneer family, the lodge is perched high above the surf. It is run by the 5th generation of that same family today.

Rates: $$

VISIT WEBSITE

Ventana Inn

Luxury spa resort with the feel of a country get-away. Think deep soaking tubs, huge windows, and stone fireplaces.

Rates: $$$$

VISIT WEBSITE

Treebones

Go glamping in Big Sur! Stay in a yurt or BYO tent and sleep in a campsite under the stars. Don't forget to try the on-site sushi bar.

Rates: $ to $$

Exploring

In and around Big Sur.

Julia Pfeiffer Burns State Park

Stroll to McWay Falls, a waterfall tumbling into the ocean.

Carmel-by-the-Sea

Visit quaint Carmel-by-the-Sea for a walk on the beach, hidden courtyards, and speciality shops .

Santa Lucia Wine Country

Forge your own wine trail through the many vineyards in the Santa Lucia Highlands.

Bixby Creek Bridge

See the iconic concrete arch as part of a scenic drive on Highway 1.
